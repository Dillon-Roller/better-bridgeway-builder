Curbside Commercial Loading Zone

Providing a curbside commercial loading zone provides many benefits:
  • Emergency Vehicles can use the center lane without conflict
  • Delivery drivers are safer unloading at the curb than the center lane
  • Drivers can see pedestrians crossing the street
  • Avoids blocking vehicle or bicycle lanes
  • Delivery drivers no longer violate California Vehicle Code 21460.5
