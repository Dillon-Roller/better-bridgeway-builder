Passing in the Center Lane is Unsafe

Cars pass the bicycles using the center turn lane so the pedestrian's refuge is lost.

Using the center lane is dangerous because oncoming cars passing from the other side may be met head on.

Passing is Unlawful

According to the California Driver Handbook:
Use the center left turn lane to prepare for and make a left turn or U-turn. It is not a regular traffic lane or passing lane.
