Better Bridgeway Builder

A Frogger style game where you help the frog cross the road and avoid getting squashed.

Complete levels, earn grant money and install new infrastructure to improve the road for pedestrians, bicyclists, drivers, and commercial deliveries.

Controls

Desktop: Use the arrow keys (← ↑ → ↓) to move the frog.
Desktop: Click on the screen where you want the frog to go.
Mobile: Touch where you want the frog to go.
