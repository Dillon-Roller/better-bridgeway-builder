Better Bridgeway Builder

A Frogger style game where you help the frog cross Bridgeway Blvd. in Sausalito and avoid getting squashed. Complete levels to gain better infrastructure and improve the road for pedestrians, bicyclists, drivers, and commercial deliveries.

Rules

  • Don't get squashed by cars, bikes, ambulances, delivery trucks.
  • Reach the top sidewalk to advance to the next level.
  • Get squashed 3 times to fail and advance to the next level.

Controls

Desktop: Use the arrow keys (← ↑ → ↓) to move the frog.
Desktop: Click on the screen where you want the frog to go.
Mobile: Touch where you want the frog to go.
