Better Bridgeway Builder

Emergency Vehicles Blocked by Center Lane Deliveries

Larger vehicles parked in the center lane complicate passage for large emergency vehicles like fire trucks and ambulances that must use Bridgeway to service the south end of Sausalito.

Hint

When an ambulance periodically appears, all other vehicles pull over. The ambulance will pass when a lane has enough clearance, but will crash into the obstacle in its path if no clear path can be found.

Nobody wins when an ambulance is blocked.
